I am honored to be selected as a winner of the **2023 Qualcomm Innovation Fellowship - North America**.

The Qualcomm Innovation Fellowship (QIF) is a highly competitive program that recognizes outstanding PhD students pursuing innovative research in mobile and related technologies. Winners receive a fellowship grant and mentorship from Qualcomm engineers.

My fellowship proposal focused on research in unary computing for energy-efficient AI accelerators.
